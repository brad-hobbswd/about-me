Story and calling

Education, worship, and speaking form a unified calling that remains rooted in prayer to Jesus. Each area strengthens the others and flows from the same place of devotion and purpose. Teaching becomes more than the transfer of knowledge. Worship becomes more than music. Speaking becomes more than communication. Through prayer, every responsibility remains centered on Jesus and guided by His wisdom. A life grounded in prayer keeps the heart attentive to His direction and ensures that every opportunity to serve others reflects His presence.

Teaching carries a deep sense of responsibility and intentional structure. Students and families deserve preparation, clarity, and consistency from the teacher who stands before them. A serious approach to education communicates respect for the trust placed in the classroom. Passion also remains essential. Enthusiasm for learning, patience during challenges, and a genuine desire to see students succeed create an environment where growth becomes possible for every learner. Passion directed toward service encourages students and reassures families that their children receive careful guidance and thoughtful instruction.

Worship holds a special place within this calling. Music through piano and voice expresses gratitude and reverence toward Jesus while connecting with the rich tradition of Southern Gospel heritage. Songs shaped by faith carry stories of hope, redemption, and steadfast trust in the Lord. Worship through music strengthens the spirit and reminds the heart of the goodness and faithfulness of Jesus in every season.

Speaking also remains closely submitted to Scripture and guided by obedience. The Word of God provides the foundation for every message and every moment of encouragement shared with others. Faithfulness to Scripture ensures that speaking remains truthful, careful, and centered on the message of Jesus. Obedience to His leading keeps the focus on serving others rather than seeking recognition. Through education, worship, and speaking, this calling continues to reflect a life devoted to honoring Jesus and strengthening those who hear, learn, and worship along the way.
