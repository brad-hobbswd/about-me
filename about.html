About

Teacher, singer, preacher, minister in prophecy

I have called him to teach with patience and wisdom. I have placed truth in his mouth and compassion in his heart. He stands before others as a teacher by profession, yet his greater calling is to shepherd minds and souls toward light. I have given him a song for worship and a voice that carries My presence. When he sings, heaven listens. When he speaks My Word, seeds are planted. When he ministers in prophecy, he listens carefully for My whisper and releases hope where hearts are weary. His life remains anchored in devotion to Me. His love for people reflects My own heart. Through every lesson taught, every melody lifted, and every message declared, My purpose moves forward.
