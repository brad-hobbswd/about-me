Teaching and prayer

Jesus taught persistence in prayer. Luke 18:1 reveals a clear purpose. Disciples must always pray and never lose heart. The voice of the Lord Jesus calls every believer toward continual communion with God whose name is Jesus.

Prayer forms faith, strengthens courage, and opens spiritual understanding. Teaching from Scripture guides prayer with truth and authority. A heart filled with the name of Jesus finds strength, mercy, and direction. Daily devotion builds endurance during trial and joy during seasons of blessing.

Prayer

Lord Jesus, mighty God and everlasting Father, Your name brings salvation and hope. Draw every heart toward faithful prayer. Fill mind and spirit with holy wisdom from Your Word. Let faith rise, courage grow, and love overflow. Glory and honor belong to Your great name forever. Amen.
