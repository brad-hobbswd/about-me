Speaking Ministry

Every Message Begins In Prayer

Biblical teaching begins long before anyone stands behind a pulpit. Every message is first shaped through prayer, careful study of Scripture, and a desire to faithfully communicate God's Word with humility and conviction.

Whether speaking during a worship service, Bible study, conference, retreat, leadership gathering, or devotional meeting, my goal remains unchanged: to glorify Jesus Christ and encourage believers through biblical truth.

Rather than relying upon personal opinion or motivational speaking, every message seeks to remain faithful to Scripture while offering practical application for everyday Christian living.

"Preach the word; be instant in season, out of season; reprove, rebuke, exhort with all longsuffering and doctrine."
2 Timothy 4:2

Ministry Opportunities

Serving Wherever God Opens The Door

Every invitation is viewed as an opportunity to faithfully serve the Kingdom of God. Whether speaking to a small Bible study or a larger congregation, the focus remains on proclaiming Jesus Christ through Scripture.

I welcome opportunities to encourage churches, ministry groups, educators, leadership teams, conferences, retreats, men's ministries, and community gatherings.

Each message is prayerfully prepared to meet the needs of the audience while remaining faithful to God's Word.

Core Values

What Guides Every Message

These principles shape every opportunity to teach, encourage, and serve.

Biblical Authority

Scripture remains the final authority for every message, every lesson, and every point of application.

Grace and Truth

Biblical truth is always presented with humility, compassion, and Christlike love.

Faithful Service

Every opportunity to speak is viewed as an act of service that belongs to Jesus Christ rather than personal recognition.
