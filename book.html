The Story Behind The Book

Why I Wrote Who Made You Judge?

This book was born out of a desire to encourage believers to approach one another with humility instead of pride and grace instead of condemnation. Throughout Scripture we are reminded that truth should never be separated from love, and love should never abandon truth.

My hope is that readers will pause long enough to examine their own hearts before judging the journey of someone else. Every chapter is an invitation to draw closer to Jesus Christ and allow His Word to transform our attitudes, conversations, and relationships.

Rather than simply presenting opinions, this book invites readers into honest reflection through biblical teaching, practical application, and the hope that only Christ provides.

From The Author
