Featured Book

Who Made You Judge?

Judgment has become one of the greatest stumbling blocks within the Church. This book gently challenges believers to exchange criticism for compassion, pride for humility, and condemnation for biblical grace.

Through Scripture, personal reflection, and practical application, each chapter encourages readers to examine their own hearts before judging someone else's journey while continually pointing back to Jesus Christ.

If grace transformed us, then grace should also shape how we respond to others.

A Life of Worship

Worship has always been more than music. It is a daily surrender of the heart before Jesus Christ, allowing every song, every prayer, and every moment of ministry to become an offering of gratitude.

Whether sitting behind the piano, singing before a congregation, or quietly worshiping in prayer, my desire remains the same: to glorify Jesus Christ and encourage others to draw near to Him.
