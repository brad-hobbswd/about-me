Quiet strength

Worship grows from a quiet and devoted life of prayer. Private moments with Jesus shape the heart long before any song rises in public.

Music then flows naturally as an expression of that devotion. Songs offered through voice or piano become a reflection of the relationship already formed with Jesus in prayer.
