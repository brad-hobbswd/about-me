Quiet strength

Worship grows from a quiet and devoted life of prayer. Private moments with Jesus shape the heart long before any song rises in public. A prayer closet becomes the place where faith deepens, humility forms, and strength develops in the presence of the Lord. In that sacred stillness, devotion matures and a life of worship begins to take root.

Music then flows naturally as an expression of that devotion. Songs offered through voice or piano become a reflection of the relationship already formed with Jesus in prayer. Worship through music carries sincerity when the heart has first bowed before Him in quiet reverence. True worship does not begin on a platform. True worship begins in prayer and continues as a living expression of love for Jesus.
