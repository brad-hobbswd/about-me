Family Dedication

A home anchored in prayer, love, and spiritual covering.

“As for me and my house, we will serve the Lord.”
Joshua 24:15

Dedication

Family life deserves prayer, protection, and steady love centered on Jesus.

Fatherhood and partnership stand at the center of daily life. Gabriel and Justin are not additions to the journey. They are the heart of it. Love within this home reflects a shared need for one another, grounded in a deeper need for Jesus. Strength, patience, and joy continue to grow through that connection.

Responsibility brings purpose. Presence brings stability. Prayer covers every moment, creating a home where love remains consistent and faith continues to lead.

Gabriel

A son deeply loved, guided with intention, and covered in prayer through every stage of growth.

Justin

A partner honored with love, faithfulness, and unity shaped through prayer and shared life.

Prayer

“The Lord bless thee, and keep thee...”
Numbers 6:24

Lord Jesus, You are the center of this home. Cover Gabriel and Justin with peace, strength, and protection. Let love remain steady, let patience grow, and let every moment reflect Your presence. Keep this home grounded in truth, guided by Your Spirit, and held together through Your name.

Rooted in prayer. Centered in Jesus.
