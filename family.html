Family

A Home Rooted in Prayer.
Strengthened by Love.

Before the classroom, before the piano, before the writing desk, and before the platform, my first calling is my home. Family is one of God's greatest gifts, and I strive to lead it with love, humility, faithfulness, and prayer through Jesus Christ.

Home First

The Greatest Ministry Begins At Home

Every opportunity God has entrusted to me begins here. Before teaching children, writing devotionals, leading worship, or speaking publicly, I am called to love, lead, and serve my family well.

Gabriel and Justin are among God's greatest blessings in my life. They remind me daily that ministry is not confined to a platform or a classroom. It begins in ordinary moments of love, encouragement, patience, forgiveness, and prayer.

Our home is not perfect, but it is continually being shaped by God's grace. Together we seek to keep Jesus Christ at the center of our decisions, our relationships, and our future.

Our Foundation

Built Upon God's Word

Every healthy home needs a firm foundation. For our family, that foundation is Jesus Christ and His unchanging Word.

"As for me and my house, we will serve the Lord."
Joshua 24:15

Gabriel

Gabriel is one of God's greatest blessings in my life. Every season brings new opportunities to encourage him, teach him God's truth, celebrate his growth, and surround him with unconditional love and prayer.

Justin

Justin is my partner in life and one of God's precious gifts. Through faithfulness, grace, patience, and prayer, we continue building a home centered upon Jesus Christ and strengthened through His love.

Our Home

A Christ centered home is built one prayer at a time. We desire our home to be a place of peace, encouragement, forgiveness, worship, and continual dependence upon Jesus Christ.

Family Dedication

A Home Committed To Jesus Christ

Family is one of the greatest responsibilities God gives us. Every conversation, every challenge, every celebration, and every ordinary day becomes another opportunity to reflect His love.

I pray our home continually reflects kindness, humility, forgiveness, compassion, and unwavering faith. While none of us are perfect, God's grace faithfully carries us through every season.

May every decision we make honor Jesus Christ, every word we speak build one another up, and every season remind us that God remains faithful.

"The Lord bless thee, and keep thee: The Lord make his face shine upon thee, and be gracious unto thee."
Numbers 6:24–25
Prayer

A Prayer For Our Home

Every day begins and ends with gratitude for God's faithfulness. This prayer reflects the desire of my heart for our family.

Lord Jesus, thank You for the gift of family. Continue to guide our home with Your wisdom, strengthen us through every season, and help us love one another with grace, patience, and humility. Let our words honor You, our decisions reflect Your truth, and our lives point others to Your goodness. Keep us rooted in prayer, faithful to Your Word, and united in Your love each day.
In Jesus' Name
A Lasting Legacy

The Greatest Legacy We Leave Is Faith

Titles, accomplishments, and careers eventually fade, but a life lived for Jesus Christ leaves an eternal legacy. My prayer is that our home continually reflects His grace, His truth, and His unfailing love for generations to come.
